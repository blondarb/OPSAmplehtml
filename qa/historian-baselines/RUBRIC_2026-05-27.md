## Post-upgrade capture instructions (run after PR is deployed)

For each sample persona on the deployed PR:
1. Reload `/consult`, pick the persona card.
2. Read the same actor briefing the pre-upgrade JSON references.
3. Run Step 1 Triage end-to-end (should still work).
4. On Step 2 Historian, click Start Voice Interview, run for 5-10 minutes.
5. End the interview. Save:
   - Full transcript (or screenshot of the transcript panel)
   - Final structured output JSON (from `historian_sessions.structured_output`)
   - Localizer differential at session end (from `localizer_results.differential`)
   - Scales administered + total scores (from `scale_results` with `consult_id` for this run)
   - Red flags surfaced (from `historian_red_flags` rows for this session)
6. Store in `qa/historian-baselines/2026-05-27-post-upgrade/{persona}.json` using the same shape as the pre-upgrade JSONs.

## Pass criteria

Because there is no pre-upgrade baseline to regress against, the post-upgrade pass criteria are absolute:

- ✅ All 5 personas reach Step 2 voice flow without error banner
- ✅ At least 4 of the 5 sessions complete (`interview_completion_status: 'complete'`, save_interview_output called)
- ✅ Localizer differential is non-empty at session end for at least 4 personas
- ✅ At least 2 of the 5 sessions trigger a `scale_step` call (Maya for MIDAS/HIT-6 is the most likely; PHQ-9 if mood symptoms surface)
- ✅ When a scale runs, items are recited verbatim from `src/lib/consult/scales/scale-library.ts` and the model paces (no item bursts) — see smoke S3
- ✅ Red flags surface where the persona briefing implies them (Maya aura, Priya optic/ascending, Rachel witnessed spells)
